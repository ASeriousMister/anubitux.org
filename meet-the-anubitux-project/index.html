Meet the Anu₿itux project

Anu₿itux is a free project that aims to provide to everyone a safe and secure enviroment to manage crypto-stuff like wallets, seeds, transactions, etc, in a secure and simple way. The Anu₿itux distro comes also with a lot of cool tutorials that will show how to use the included tools or how to approach with cryptos minimizing risks.

Stay tuned with following our Telegram channel and our Twitter profile, to receive notifications every time a new releas or a new tutorial comes out.

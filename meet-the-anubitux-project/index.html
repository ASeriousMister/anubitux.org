Meet the Anu₿itux project

Anu₿itux is a free project that aims to provide everyone with a safe and secure environment to manage crypto-stuff like wallets, seeds, transactions, etc, securely and simply. The Anu₿itux distro comes also with a lot of cool tutorials that will show how to use the included tools or how to approach cryptos minimizing risks.

Stay tuned by following our Telegram channel and our Twitter profile, to receive notifications every time a new release or a new tutorial comes out.

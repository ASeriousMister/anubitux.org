Meet the AnuBitux project

AnuBitux is a free project that aims to provide everyone with a safe and secure environment to manage crypto-stuff like wallets, seeds, transactions, etc, securely and simply. The AnuBitux distro comes also with a lot of cool tutorials that show how to use the included tools or how to approach cryptos minimizing risks.

Stay tuned by following our Telegram channel and our X profile, to receive notifications every time a new release or a new tutorial comes out.

We also have a YouTube channel to share news, video tutorials and useful tips about our project!
